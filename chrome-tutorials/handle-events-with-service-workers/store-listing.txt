Save, organize, and access your favorite links instantly from your browser's address bar

Type "ql" + keyword in your address bar. Go anywhere instantly.

No more bookmark folders. No more typing full URLs. Just instant access to every site you care about.

What You Get:

⚡ Instant Navigation
Type "ql" in your address bar, add a keyword, and press Enter. That's it. Your most-used sites are now one keystroke away. You can also type ql/<keyword> directly in the address bar — no activation step required.

🎯 Smart Suggestions
Visit a site frequently? We'll suggest creating a quick link automatically, with a keyword and description drawn from the page's tab name — not just the URL. Accept, edit, or block — you're in control. Toggle auto-suggestions on or off in Settings at any time.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
Save the current page instantly: Option+L (Mac) or Ctrl+L (Windows/Linux). Never touch your mouse.

🔍 Search Your Links
Can't remember the keyword? Search by URL, keyword, or description. Real-time filtering finds what you need — and fuzzy keyword matching surfaces links even when you only remember part of the keyword.

🚫 Manage Blocked Suggestions
Sites you've dismissed are tracked in a dedicated Blocked Suggestions view — searchable and easy to unblock whenever you change your mind.

🔒 Private & Secure
All your quick links stay on your device — no external servers, no tracking, no data collection. Your browsing patterns and saved links are yours alone.

☁️ Synced Across Devices
Your quick links automatically sync to your Google account via Chrome Sync. Access the same shortcuts on every device where you're signed in — no manual setup required.
